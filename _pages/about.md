I am an applied scientist at Amazon, working on AI agent, Large Language Model, and Natural Language Processing. Before joining Amazon, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at University of Utah, co-advised by [Prof. Ellen Riloff](https://www2.cs.arizona.edu/~riloff/index.html) and [Prof.Vivek Srikumar](https://svivek.com/). I earned my Master degree in Computer Science at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I worked with [Prof. Dragomir Radev](https://cpsc.yale.edu/people/dragomir-radev). Before it, I obtained my Bachelor degrees in Maths and Physics at University of Rochester.
